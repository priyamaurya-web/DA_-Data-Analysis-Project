ðŸ“Š Key Problem Statements and Solutions

1. Monthly Sales Trend

 Goal: Analyze how gross sales change month over month.
 Method: Line Chart Visualization in IBM Cognos.
 Insight: Shows sales growth pattern and seasonality in e-commerce transactions.

2. Revenue by Category

 Goal: Identify which product categories generate the most revenue.
 Method: Clustered Bar Chart.
 Insight: Helps prioritize inventory and marketing strategies for high-performing categories.

3. Correlation between Category and Gross Amount

 Goal: Determine whether category type influences total sales amount.
 Method: Scatter Plot Visualization.
 Insight: Visual correlation between different categories and their gross revenue contribution.

4. Status Summary Report

 Goal: Summarize order status (Delivered, Cancelled, etc.) by category and amount.
 Method: Cross-tab report.
 Insight: Provides a clear summary of successful vs. cancelled transactions across categories.

5. Most Cancelled Styles/Sizes

 Goal: Identify which product styles or sizes have the highest cancellation rates.
 Method: Filter and grouping analysis in IBM Cognos.
 Insight: Reveals unpopular product variations for better inventory management.

6. Count of Products by Category

 Goal: Determine total number of products across different categories.
 Method: Grouping, summarizing, and sorting reports.
 Insight: Displays product distribution, helping understand stock spread across categories.

7. Customer Segmentation

 Goal: Segment customers by their purchasing behavior (recency, frequency, category preference).
 Method: Grouping and sorting based on customer activity data.
 Insight: Helps identify loyal, frequent, and new customers for targeted marketing.

8. Most Purchased Size/Style/Color Combination

 Goal: Find the most popular combination of size, style, and color.
 Method: Grouping and summarizing in list report.
 Insight: Shows which product features are most appealing to customers.

9. Sales Trend by Channel (Bubble Chart)

 Goal: Analyze performance across different sales channels.
 Method: Bubble Chart with Sales Channel (X-axis), Quantity (Y-axis), and Category (Color).
 Insight: Highlights which sales platforms drive the most sales volume.

10. Channel-Wise Performance Analysis

 Goal: Evaluate how the e-commerce business performs across different channels.
 Method: Clustered Column Chart.
 Insight: Identifies top-performing and underperforming channels to optimize marketing and logistics efforts.
